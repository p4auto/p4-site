Our Commitment to EVs

At P4 Automotive, we are committed to EV awareness!  That's why we've launched a program that brings our employees to local businesses to showcase EVs and give people the opportunity to test drive them.
Employees help educate the public about different EV models, ranges and charge times, and “demystify” EV’s for the community. Attendees can drive our selection of electric vehicles, and they can ask any questions they have about making the switch, all in a relaxed, pressure free but professional environment. 

Test drives

We invite potential customers to test drive electric vehicles so they can experience the performance and benefits firsthand. We are also offering remote test drives.

Education

We provide information and resources to help customers learn about the benefits of electric vehicles and understand how they work.

Financing & Trade-ins

We offer financing and leasing options to make it easy for customers to purchase or lease an electric vehicle.

Charging infrastructure

We have installed charging stations at our dealership and are working with local businesses to provide additional charging options for electric vehicle owners.

Looking to the Future

P4 Automotive is committed to advancing the sale and adoption of electric vehicles. We installed one of the first publicly available charging stations in our community, free of charge and we are dedicated to doing our part to accelerate EV adoption.

To support this transition, we have made EVs a focus in our inventory and stocking efforts. We are committed to offering a wide range of electric vehicle models to our customers and to providing the necessary resources and support to help them make the switch to an electric vehicle.

We understand that the adoption of EVs requires a concerted effort from all sectors of society, and we are committed to playing our part. We believe that by working together, we can help drive the adoption of electric vehicles.

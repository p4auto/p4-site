Our Team

At P4 Automotive, we believe that our people are our most valuable asset, and we believe that a good leader inspires great employees. Our diverse leadership team brings a wealth of experience to their roles as they set the tone for P4 Automotive.

Jeroyl Shelley

Controller

Jeroyl Shelley is the Controller at P4 Auto, where she leads the accounting team and is responsible for the overall financial management of the company. She is responsible for developing and maintaining the company's financial systems and controls, overseeing the preparation of financial statements and reports, and managing the budgeting and forecasting process. She also works closely with other departments to provide financial support and analysis, and ensure compliance with all financial regulations and laws.

Susan Jones

HR Director

Susan Jones is the HR Director at P4, where she leads the human resources department and is responsible for the overall management of human resources for the organization. She is responsible for developing and implementing policies and procedures, managing employee relations and recruitment, and ensuring compliance with legal and regulatory requirements.

Robert Denton

Marketing Director

As the Marketing Director, Robert brings his years of experience in dealership sales and management, as well as his experience as an Advertising Consultant for Autotrader, as a Sales representative for DDC and his experience as a KBB Representative. His proficiencies with automotive software in and out of the dealership allow him to leverage all the dealership data points to better drive ROI. He leads a team of marketing professionals who have brought video production, SEO and website management in house.  

Emily Hill

Customer Experience Director

Emily Hill, the Customer Experience Director at P4, is an expert in customer service and experience. With her extensive experience, she is proficient with CSI guidelines for multiple brands and is responsible for developing and implementing strategies to improve customer satisfaction, managing customer feedback and concerns and training employees on best practices for customer service. 

Jordan Maus

New Inventory Director

Jordan is the New Inventory Director at P4 Auto where he is directly responsible for new vehicle ordering and turn. Jordan utilizes a wide range of sales and market data to stock the correct inventory, price it to move, and increase our allocation.  

Aaron Wildey

Used Inventory Director

Aaron Wildey leads the used vehicle acquisition department. His team buys vehicles- from auctions, service customers or from other sources. Aaron’s team directly prices used inventory based on the latest market trends, actively managing inventory levels and turn rate based on the latest financial data.
